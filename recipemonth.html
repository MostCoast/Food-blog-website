Recipe of the month

Vegetarian goulash - Starterkit

Ingredients:

  • 2 red onions
  • 2-4 cloves of garlic
  • 500-1000g potatos
  • 3 carrots
  • 2 paprikas
  • 500g vegetable of choice
  • 3 teaspoons of smoked paprika powder
  • 3 teaspoons of normal paprika powder
  • 1 teaspoon caraway (full, not grinded!)
  • 1 tablespoon marjoram
  • 1 tablespoon thyme
  • 1/2 tablespoon lovage
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1,5l vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar

How to do it:

  1. Chop vegetables.
  2. Take a big cooking pot and fry the onions/paprikas until they are glassy.
  3. Add garlic and fry 2 min.
  4. Add the normal and the smoked paprika powder and let it stir shortly.
  5. Add the vinegar/marjoram/thyme/ lovage, let it stir shortly.
  6. Add the rest of the vegetables and the vegetable broth.
  7. Add the caraway and the bay leaves.
  8. Let it boil for at least 30 min (the longer the tastier) on low/medium heat.
